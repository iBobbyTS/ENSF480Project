VIP Benefits

As a VIP with AcmePlex, you enjoy a premium movie-going experience with exclusive benefits and privileges:

  • No Administrative Cancellation Fee: Registered Users can cancel their tickets up to 72 hours prior to the show and receive a full credit for future purchases without incurring the standard 15% administrative fee.
  • Early Access to Tickets: Gain early access to movie showtimes and purchase tickets before they are made available to the general public. Up to 10% of the seats for each show are reserved exclusively for Registered Users, offered on a first-come, first-serve basis.
  • Exclusive News and Updates: Be the first to know! Registered Users receive movie news and announcements before they are shared with the public, including details about upcoming releases, special events, and promotional offers.
  • Convenience with Saved Information: Registered Users have their personal information, including name, address, and payment details (credit or debit card), securely stored in the system, making ticket purchases quick and hassle-free.
  • Annual Membership: All these benefits are available for a nominal annual membership fee of $20.00, ensuring you enjoy a seamless and enhanced movie-going experience with AcmePlex.

By becoming a Registered User, you not only avoid additional fees and delays but also gain access to a host of exclusive perks that elevate your movie experience.
